☁️ Cloudinary Setup (one-time, free)

Photos are stored on Cloudinary's free CDN — not Vercel. Zero storage cost on your hosting.
1. Sign up free at cloudinary.com → get your Cloud Name
2. Go to Settings → Upload → Add upload preset → set to Unsigned → copy preset name
3. Enter both below and click Save — then you can upload photos directly here.
